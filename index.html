What is Info Concerts ?

Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of the Parisian music scene with our website, a platform dedicated to keeping you informed of all the concerts taking place in the French capital. Whether you're a jazz fan, an indie rock fan, a classical music enthusiast or simply looking for new and emerging talent, our site is your ideal companion for exploring the musical diversity of Paris.


A complete overview

Imagine having access to a complete overview of all the concerts in Paris, just a click away. Our site lists a multitude of musical events, from small intimate venues to large amphitheatres, giving you a global perspective on the richness of the Parisian music scene. Whether you're looking for open-air concerts in the city's picturesque parks, acoustic performances in cosy cafés or electrifying shows in legendary venues, you'll find all the information you need to plan your next musical outing.




Stay connected and informed

To make sure you never miss a concert, subscribe to our newsletter and follow us on social networks. We'll keep you up to date with the latest music news, exclusive concert announcements and special offers reserved for subscribers.


Your Musical Adventure Begins Here

Whether you're a long-time Parisian or a casual visitor looking for an authentic musical experience, our site is your gateway to the captivating world of live music in the City of Light. So get ready to experience unforgettable moments, discover new sounds and let yourself be carried away by the bewitching rhythm of Paris in music. Welcome to Info Concert and let the adventure begin !
